how to play
the race

Five competitors race across four stages. In each stage, a competitor can catch the one directly ahead — bumping them down and taking their place for the next stage.

Competitors have four attributes — Power, Agility, Stamina and Tactics — which determine how well they perform in each race type. Check race hints to see which attributes matter most in each stage.

your prediction

Tap a dot on the bump chart to select a competitor. Then tap ▲ above their dot to predict they bump up, or ▼ below to predict they get bumped down. Tap × to remove a prediction.

Races lock in consecutively from Race 1 — so Race 2 can only lock once Race 1 is correct. You have four attempts to predict all four races perfectly.

scoring

After each submission you'll see how many positions you got right per race. The goal is to solve it in as few attempts as possible. The final screen reveals the correct bump chart so you can see where your prediction went wrong.
